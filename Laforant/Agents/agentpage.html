OUR AGENTS

ThAnAtos

Introducing Thanatos, a personification of death, she can poison her enemy by drawing out her smoke and she can also double jump. Thanatos agility is top-notch, you should be careful and aware when facing her.

VoLt

In 1820 there was an experiment for gaining superhuman power. Volt was the test subject in Umasa Laboratory. The experiment was a success and now Volt has tremendous power. She can shoot fire from her hands and also has superhuman reaction.

CAINE

Caine spent her entire life meditating and doing intense training with her grandfather. She wants to bring eternal peace to the world by joining the SEAL Army. Caine has sixth senses to keep track of the enemy and she can make herself invisible.

YAldabAoth

Before the galaxy was created, there was once a ruler called Yaldabaoth who came from a supernova. Now, he wants to seek chaos on earth. Yaldabaoth can make himself immortal for 3 seconds and can also do some magical powers.
